# Monetization Brainstorm — 2026-03-04

## Key Findings

### Subscription is not a good fit for lottery content
- Data is public and free
- Low-frequency content (a few draws/week)
- Hard to gate behind paywall

### What top lottery sites do

| Site | Traffic | Revenue Model | Key Feature |
|---|---|---|---|
| LotteryPost.com | #1 US lottery site | Premium membership ~$8/mo | **Community forum** (400K+ posts) |
| LotteryUSA.com | 1.4M visits/mo | Ads | Results checking as daily habit |
| LottoStrategies.com | Unknown | Premium tools | Wheeling systems, smart picks |
| Jackpocket/TheLotter | Large | Transaction fee on ticket purchases | Lottery pool/syndicate play |

### What drives repeat visits
1. **Results checking** — after every draw (habit-forming)
2. **Community discussion** — predictions, stories, strategies
3. **Jackpot hype** — viral traffic spikes at $500M+

### What drives subscription revenue
- Exclusive analysis tools (LotteryPost: Deflate, Inspector)
- "Smart" number generators (LottoStrategies)
- Community features (premium blogs, messaging)
